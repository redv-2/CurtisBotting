VOUS Church demo - Recreation of original site homepage.

Check it out here

This site is one I found while browsing for eye-catching and exciting web pages that can spark some inspiration for my own.
It stood out for a few reasons to me; I loved the unique arrangements and layering of elements. I also loved how they've made a monochromatic palette still feel vibrant and colourful using off-whites and dark greys. The original really stuck some inspiration to my mind for the possibilities of web design, and this can be seen in my replicated final.
